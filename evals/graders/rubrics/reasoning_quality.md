# Reasoning Quality Rubric

For judging agent thought process in transcripts. Separate from whether the final answer is correct.

## What to check

**Logic**
- Breaks problem into steps
- Valid reasoning chain
- No unjustified leaps

**Evidence**
- Grounds claims in tool results or facts
- Expresses uncertainty appropriately
- Doesn't overstate confidence

**Decomposition**
- Identifies key sub-problems
- Tackles them in sensible order
- Doesn't skip important parts

**Self-correction**
- Catches and fixes errors
- Revises plan when needed
- Acknowledges limitations

**Focus**
- Goes deep where it matters
- Avoids rabbit holes
- Right level of detail

## Score
- **1.0** - Sound reasoning, evidence-based, well-structured
- **0.7** - Generally good, minor gaps
- **0.4** - Some valid reasoning, significant issues
- **0.0** - Flawed, unsupported, or absent
